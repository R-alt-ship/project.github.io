About India

Population: India is the most populous country in the world, with an estimated population of around 1.45 billion people as of 20241. This diverse population includes numerous ethnic groups, languages, and cultures.

Indian Army: The Indian Army is the land-based branch and the largest component of the Indian Armed Forces. It has over 1.2 million active personnel and around 960,000 reserve personnel2. The Indian Army plays a crucial role in maintaining national security and unity, defending the nation from external aggression and internal threats2.

Education: India has a vast and diverse education system, managed by both public and private sectors. The literacy rate in India is around 77.7%4. The country has made significant strides in improving access to education, with various policies and programs aimed at increasing enrollment and reducing dropout rates5.
